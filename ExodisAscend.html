Exodis Ascend

Overview

The game is inspired by traditional Fantasy RPGs. It will follow the adventure of the player (the hero), on their quest to reach the top of the Grand Tower Exodis.

The player will experience a story where they will be faced with events that assist or hinder their progress. These events will introduce unique NPCs that will build to the storytelling of the adventure with the option of recruiting some of them into the player’s party. These companions will have unique fighting abilities, allowing the player to create their favored playstyle.

To achieve replayability value, the game will include a significant amount of events and characters. This is so the second playthrough will feel fresh in terms of character interactions, events and combat strategy.

Features

  • Randomised Event
  • Turn-Based RPG Combat System
  • Short storyline with replayability rate
