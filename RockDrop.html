Rock Drop

Concept

React and click the rock as it drops.

Summary

I decided to make a simple game using the water splash particle that I have done in class. Initially it is just showing a water splash particle, then I expanded on it by having a rock dropping and the player is to destroy it (click it) before it drops into the water that shows the water splash particle.
